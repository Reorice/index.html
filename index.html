'A museum of art interwoven'



About

A prestigious unit of fine arts led by Itsuki Shu.

They're a group with their own, distinct perspective on the world and retain a highly individual, inflexible distance when interacting with their fans.

Their media work primarily focuses on capitalizing on their artistic attributes, such as participating in talk shows as guests, acting as narrators for fine arts programs, and appearing in movies and plays.

Their theme color is wine red.
